About the Platform

Your Smart Urban Gardening Companion

Smart Baghichaa is a web-based smart gardening assistant developed as a Final Year Project at SAGE University, Indore. It helps urban residents cultivate and maintain plants within limited spaces — bridging the gap between complex agricultural systems and everyday plant care.

🌿

Data-Driven Recommendation Engine

Analyses location, season, and space using a curated plant database to suggest the most suitable plants for every user.

🔔

Personalised Care & Reminders

Automated daily reminders for watering, fertilising, and sunlight ensure healthy plant growth.

📊

Growth Tracking Module

Users log progress updates; the system generates dynamic suggestions based on actual growth data.

🤝

Community Feedback Section

Plant parents share tips and experiences, fostering peer-to-peer learning across the platform.

General Care Tips
💧

Watering Schedule

Custom watering frequency based on plant species, pot size, and local humidity levels.

  • Tulsi: Every 2 days in summer
  • Cherry Tomatoes: Daily during fruiting
  • Succulents: Once a week
  • Spinach / Palak: Every 1–2 days
  • Marigold: Every 2–3 days
🌾

Fertiliser Reminders

Timely nutrient applications to keep your plants growing strong and healthy.

  • Compost: Every 2–3 weeks
  • Liquid feed: Every 10 days
  • Nitrogen boost in growth phase
  • Potassium before flowering
  • Avoid over-feeding succulents
☀️

Sunlight Requirements

Optimise plant placement based on the light exposure available in your space.

  • Full sun (6+ hrs): Tomatoes, Marigold
  • Partial sun (3–6 hrs): Tulsi, Coriander
  • Low light: Pothos, Peace Lily
  • Rotate indoor pots weekly
  • Avoid harsh afternoon sun in summer
🪴

Soil & Potting Tips

The right soil mix makes a dramatic difference in plant health and growth rate.

  • Use well-draining potting mix
  • Add perlite for drainage
  • Repot every 12–18 months
  • Check pH: 6.0–7.0 ideal for most
  • Mulch outdoor beds to retain moisture
🐛

Pest & Disease Control

Early identification and organic remedies to protect your plants naturally.

  • Neem oil spray: weekly preventive
  • Check leaf undersides for pests
  • Remove infected leaves promptly
  • Companion planting deters pests
  • Avoid overwatering to prevent fungus
🌡️

Seasonal Adjustments

Adapt your care routine as temperatures and rainfall change through the year.

  • Reduce watering in winter
  • Shade cloth in peak summer
  • Monsoon: improve pot drainage
  • Move frost-sensitive plants indoors
  • Increase feeding in spring
